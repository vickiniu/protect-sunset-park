Protect Sunset Park

Demand that the City Council says NO to Industry City rezoning.

Real estate developers and the lawmakers that bow to them are trying to rezone over a million square feet of Industry City without community agreement and support.

They want to transform Industry City from the largest working-class industrial waterfront in Brooklyn into luxury hotels, retail stores, and corporate offices — we know this will only hike up rents and cause even more displacement in Sunset Park.

We are calling on the City Council to vote NO on rezoning Industry City.
